Welcome... I think

Ok, so lets do this

Hey, so I am trying something new, I am actually hoping that I will maintain a blog with some bits and bobs on it that I see and do, make and find, or whatever, I am going to do this with Ghost running on my Mac Book, then using Buster to deploy the flat version of the site to GitHub Pages - what this should allow is for a pretty robust and secure site, without much potential for things to go horribly wrong... famous last words, but we'll see.

If I manage to keep this up for any length of time, I might actually go to the trouble of sorting out a proper domain for it, but lets not get ahead of ourselves shall we?

I just thought I would put a photo of my buddy Chris Jackson scoring a diving touchdown that I took the other day against South Wales Warriors, its not really relevant but damn son, I so often miss the action that when I don't I have to share the wealth!

Till next time...
